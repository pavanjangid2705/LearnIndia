⚖️ Law

📘About the Course

▫️Law courses focus on legal systems, justice, and rights. These courses prepare students for careers in legal practice and public service.

🧩Popular Specializations

  • BA LLB
  • BBA LLB
  • Corporate Law
  • Criminal Law
  • Cyber Law

🎓Eligibility

  • 10+2 in any stream (CLAT or other entrance exams)

⏳ Course Duration

  • Integrated Law (BA LLB): 5 Years
  • LLB after graduation: 3 Years

🛠 Skills Required

  • Communication & argument skills
  • Logical reasoning
  • Critical thinking
  • Confidence

💼Career Opportunities

  • Lawyer
  • Legal Advisor
  • Judge
  • Public Prosecutor
  • Corporate Legal Executive

📈 Higher Studies

  • LLM
  • Judicial Services
  • Legal Research

💸 Average Salary

  • Junior Lawyer: ₹3,00,000 – ₹6,00,000 per year
  • Legal Advisor / Corporate Lawyer: ₹5,00,000 – ₹12,00,000 per year
  • Judge / Government Legal Roles: ₹7,00,000+ per year

🎓 Top Colleges / Universities

  • National Law School of India University (NLSIU), Bengaluru – Top law school in India year after year.
  • National Academy of Legal Studies and Research (NALSAR), Hyderabad – Excellent legal programs (commonly top-ranked).
  • The WB National University of Juridical Sciences (NUJS), Kolkata – Strong reputation for law.
