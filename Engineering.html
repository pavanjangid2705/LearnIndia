🔧Engineering



📘 About the Course:

▫️Engineering focuses on applying science and technology to design, build and maintain machines, structures, software and systems.


🧩 Popular Specializations

  • Computer Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication

🎓 Eligibility

  • 10+2 with Science (PCM)
  • Entrance exams (if applicable)

⏳ Course Duration

  • Diploma: 3 years
  • Degree (B.Tech / BE): 4 years

🛠 Skills Required

  • Problem-solving
  • Logical thinking
  • Creativity
  • Technical knowledge

💼 Career Options

  • Software Engineer
  • Civil Engineer
  • Mechanical Engineer
  • Data Analyst
  • Government Jobs

📈 Higher Studies

  • M.Tech / MS
  • MBA
  • Research

💸 Average Salary

  • Freshers: ₹3–6 LPA (varies by field)
  • Mid-Level: ₹6,00,000 – ₹12,00,000 per year
  • Experienced: ₹12,00,000+ per year

🎓 Top Colleges / Universities

  • Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT Madras) – Ranked #1 in India for engineering by NIRF.
  • Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay) – Another top IIT with strong placements & research.
  • Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi) – Highly reputed globally and in India.
  • Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani (BITS Pilani) – Known for excellent engineering programs (private).
