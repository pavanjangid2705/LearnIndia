💼 Commerce

📘About the Course

▫️Commerce courses focus on business, finance, accounting, and economics. These courses are ideal for students interested in corporate and financial careers.

🧩Popular Specializations

  • B.Com (General / Honours)
  • Accounting & Finance
  • Banking & Insurance
  • Economics
  • Business Analytics

🎓Eligibility

  • 10+2 with Commerce (other streams may apply)

⏳ Course Duration

  • Bachelor’s Degree (B.Com): 3 Years

🛠 Skills Required

  • Numerical ability
  • Analytical skills
  • Business understanding
  • Attention to detail

💼Career Opportunities

  • Accountant
  • Banker
  • Financial Analyst
  • Business Consultant
  • Tax Advisor

📈 Higher Studies

  • M.Com
  • MBA
  • CA / CS / CMA

💸 Average Salary

  • Accountant: ₹2,50,000 – ₹5,00,000 per year
  • Financial Analyst: ₹3,50,000 – ₹7,00,000 per year
  • Banking: ₹2,50,000 – ₹6,00,000 per year
  • Business Consultant: ₹4,00,000 – ₹10,00,000 per year

🎓 Top Colleges / Universities

  • Shri Ram College of Commerce (SRCC), Delhi University – One of the most reputed commerce colleges.
  • Hindu College, Delhi University – Strong commerce & economics programs.
  • Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI), Delhi – Good commerce & business studies.
