🩺 Medical

📘 About the Course

▫️Medical courses focus on healthcare, diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases. These courses are ideal for students interested in saving lives and improving public health.

🧩 Popular Specializations

  • MBBS
  • BDS (Dental)
  • Nursing
  • Pharmacy
  • Physiotherapy
  • Medical Laboratory Technology

🎓 Eligibility

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB)
  • Entrance exams like NEET

⏳ Course Duration

  • MBBS: 5.5 years
  • Nursing / Pharmacy: 3–4 years

🛠 Skills Required

  • Patience and empathy
  • Strong memory
  • Observation skills
  • Dedication and discipline

💼 Career Opportunities

  • Doctor
  • Dentist
  • Nurse
  • Pharmacist
  • Medical Officer

📈 Higher Studies

  • MD / MS
  • Specialization courses
  • Research & medical teaching

💸 Average Salary

  • Doctors (MBBS): ₹5,00,000 – ₹12,00,000+ per year
  • Nurses: ₹2,00,000 – ₹5,00,000 per year
  • Pharmacists: ₹2,50,000 – ₹5,50,000 per year

🎓 Top Colleges / Universities

  • All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Delhi – Usually #1 for MBBS & medical studies in India.
  • Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham (Amrita), Coimbatore – Highly ranked for medical and allied health programs.
  • Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), Manipal – Well known for medical programs.
